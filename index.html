Your Receiver, Your Data, Zero Internet

ADSBWho connects directly to your personal ADS-B receiver on your local Wi-Fi network. No cloud, no data usage, no latency — just instant aircraft data straight from your antenna.

Compatible with FlightAware PiAware, ADS-B Exchange feeders, and any receiver running dump1090 or readsb.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. ADSBWho is designed with privacy at its core.

Location Data: Your location is used solely to calculate aircraft proximity, compass direction, and moon transit predictions. Location data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

ADS-B Data: When using a local receiver, all data stays on your local network. Remote feed queries are made directly to the data provider — ADSBWho does not operate any intermediate servers.

Spotting Log & Statistics: All logged data (aircraft sightings, watchlists, coverage data) is stored locally on your device. No personal data is collected, shared, or sold.

Analytics: ADSBWho does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Contact: For questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the app's App Store page.
